Key Decision — Payment on Behalf?

✅ Correct Payment (Same Entity)

  • HK Co. pays HK expense directly
  • Pay from own bank account
  • No intercompany entry needed

⚠️ Payment on Behalf (Different Entity)

  • Use Payment Request Form
  • Record as Due From between companies
  • Reconcile & recharge at month-end only
Payment Control Process — No PR, No Purchase. No Voucher, No Cash.
1
Purchase Request (PR)
Requester submits PR with details & justification
→
2
Approval
Required approvers review based on authority matrix
→
3
Purchase Order (PO)
PO issued for ≥10,000 THB after PR approval
→
4
Payment Voucher (PV)
Submit PV with all supporting documents
→
5
Payment
Payment made only after all required approvals
PR Required for all expenses ≥ 10,000 THB. Splitting payments to avoid approval limits is strictly prohibited. All vouchers must have complete supporting documents.
Required Documents by Stage

PR (Purchase Request)

  • Justification
  • Budget / Project reference
  • Required approvals

PO (Purchase Order)

  • Vendor details
  • Amount
  • Terms & Conditions
  • Delivery Details

Payment Voucher (PV)

  • Invoice / Receipt
  • Contract / Agreement
  • PR & PO (if applicable)
  • Tax Invoice (if any)
Cash Advance Process — 3 Steps
1
Request & Approve
Submit Cash Advance Form 3–5 business days before funds needed
→
2
Spend & Collect
Use for approved purpose only. Collect official Tax Invoices / Receipts
→
3
Clear & Settle
Submit Reconciliation Form within 7 business days. Return remaining cash.
⚠️ Unsettled Advances Policy: Any outstanding balance after 7 business days will be automatically transferred to the employee's personal account for payroll adjustment.
Critical Rules

Cash Advance Form Must Include

  • Itemized cost estimation
  • Project / business purpose
  • Dates of usage
  • Approval per authority matrix

❌ Not Accepted

  • Generic cash leaflets
  • Receipts without correct Company Name & Tax ID
  • Late reconciliation (>7 days)
Approval Matrix by Amount (THB)
Amount (THB) Required Approvers Documents Required Payment Method
0 – 2,000 Requester + Accountant (Front Desk) + Manager None Petty Cash (With Receipt)
2,001 – 10,000 Requester + Manager + Finance Department None Bank Transfer / Petty Cash
10,001 – 50,000 PR/PO Required Requester + Manager + CFO + Finance Department Approved PR + Approved PO Bank Transfer (With Voucher)
50,001 – 100,000 PR/PO Required Requester + Manager + CFO + Finance Dept + Director (1) Approved PR + Approved PO Bank Transfer (With Voucher)
100,001 and above PR/PO Required Requester + Manager + CFO + Finance Dept + Directors (2) Approved PR + Approved PO Bank Transfer (With Voucher)
⛔ Splitting payments to avoid approval limits is strictly prohibited. All expenses of 10,000 THB and above require prior PR/PO approval before purchasing.
7 Key Rules of the New Payment System
01
No Cash Pooling
No more combining cash from different companies. Each entity keeps its own funds separate.
02
Pay from Correct Entity
Each company pays its own expenses from its own bank account.
03
Payment Request if Paying for Others
If paying on behalf of another company, use the Payment Request Form.
04
No Reimbursement Without Project
Every expense must have a company, project, and approver assigned.
05
Approval Before Payment
No approval = No payment. Follow the authority matrix.
06
Petty Cash by Entity
Petty cash is allowed but must be separate by entity with a fixed float.
07
Intercompany Only Month-End
Intercompany reconciliation and recharge are done monthly, not daily.
4Es Framework — Check Before You Spend
1
Ensure Approval
Always check the Approval Matrix and obtain proper authorization before any financial commitment, purchase, or travel booking.
2
Enter Project Code
A valid Project Code is mandatory for all expense forms. Consult your manager or Finance team if unsure.
3
Execute Correct Process
Follow: Request → Approval → Purchase/Spend → Payment/Settle. Cash advances must be cleared within 7 business days.
4
Exhibit Documents
Support every claim with valid documentation — official Tax Invoices, Receipts, and required travel documents.
"Before submitting any expense, make sure you 4Es: Ensure Approval, Enter the Code, Execute the Process, and Exhibit your Receipts!"
