Finance & Payment Policies
Internal reference — Envisage Worldwide · Last updated 2026
📋 General Rules
✈️ Travel & Per Diem
🏠 Accommodation
🧾 Expense Claims
💳 Payment Process
✕
No Prior Approval = No PurchaseAll work-related travel and purchases must be approved in advance by the staff's supervisor before any commitments or bookings are made. Retroactive approvals are not accepted except in genuine emergencies.
✕
Outstanding Advances Block New AdvancesNo new travel advances may be issued to employees who have a previous outstanding advance that has not yet been cleared.
!
Clear Advances Within 5 Working DaysCash advances must be cleared within five working days of the end of a trip. Failure to clear advances before month-end may result in delayed salary payment.
!
Original Documents RequiredOriginal tickets (air, bus, train, boat) and original hotel tax invoices must be attached. Where no receipt is available, an "Expense Without Receipt" form must be used, checked, and approved by the Finance officer.
i
Most Economical Mode of TravelStaff are encouraged to travel by the most reasonable and economical method. In genuine emergencies or for safety/security reasons, other reasonable modes of travel may be used with justification.
i
Brief Trip Report RequiredA brief trip report and all relevant supporting documents must be attached to the Travelling Expense Report upon return.
✓
Reimbursement Basis OnlyAll finance support (travel, accommodation, utilities) is provided strictly on a reimbursement basis upon submission of valid supporting documents. No pre-payment or forward reimbursement without receipts.
!
Right to Reject Unsupported ClaimsThe organisation reserves the right to reject or partially reimburse expenses deemed unreasonable, unsupported, or outside policy limits. (Mallika's recommendation — adopted.)

Rates apply to foreign staff on full-time contract. Supervisor approval via Travel Request Form required at least 1 week before departure. Submit expense report within 7 days of return.

Staff Category Lodging Ceiling / Night Daily Per Diem
Interns & Junior Staff 700 THB 500 THB
Researchers 1,000 THB 700 THB
Senior Staff / Managers 1,200 THB 900 THB

Shared Accommodation on Trips (75–80% of combined ceiling)

Combination Calculation Combined Ceiling / Night
Junior + Junior (700 + 700) × 75% 1,050 THB
Researcher + Junior (1,000 + 700) × 75% 1,275 THB
i
Per Diem Reduction When Meals ProvidedPer diem may be adjusted where meals are already provided by the hotel, conference organiser, or external host. Guideline: breakfast −20%, lunch −30%, dinner −50%. If breakfast is included in the hotel rate, lodging ceiling may exceed the standard by up to the per-diem breakfast equivalent, subject to management discretion.
!
Client / Partner Hospitality MealsBusiness meals for clients require prior approval from a Department Head or Director. Maximum 2,000 THB per person (employee + client). Original itemised receipts required with names of guests and business purpose stated. Employees may not also claim personal per diem for the same meal.
i
FlightsEconomy class for all flights. The organisation reimburses the cost of economy tickets. Employees should book in advance to secure reasonable rates.
i
High-Cost Destination FlexibilityStandard lodging ceilings may be insufficient for high-cost destinations (e.g., Bangkok, Phuket, international travel) depending on season. Higher rate tiers may be granted on a case-by-case basis with prior written management approval before booking.
✕
Non-Reimbursable Travel ExpensesAlcohol (unless specifically approved for client hospitality), minibar, room service, personal entertainment, shopping, traffic fines, and any non-business expenses will not be reimbursed.
!
Minor Local Transport Without ReceiptFor minor local transport where receipts are unavailable, employees should provide reasonable supporting details (destination, date, estimated cost) within the expense report.

Applies to foreign staff on full-time contract. All amounts are total monthly ceilings covering rent, electricity, water, internet, and other associated fees. Submit monthly claims within 15 days after month-end.

Staff Category Monthly Ceiling (All-Inclusive) Covers
Interns & Junior Staff 5,000 THB Rent + electricity + water + internet
Researchers 6,000 THB Rent + electricity + water + internet
Senior Staff / Managers 7,000 THB Rent + electricity + water + internet

Shared Accommodation (80% of combined individual ceilings)

Combination Calculation Combined Ceiling / Month
Junior + Junior (5,000 + 5,000) × 80% 8,000 THB
Researcher + Junior (6,000 + 5,000) × 80% 8,800 THB
Senior + Researcher (7,000 + 6,000) × 80% 10,400 THB
i
Employee's Responsibility to Find AccommodationThe employee is responsible for finding their own accommodation. The organisation may provide support or a list of potential properties, but the final decision and rental agreement is the employee's responsibility. If costs exceed the ceiling, the employee pays the difference.
!
Required Documents for ReimbursementSubmit a copy of the signed rental agreement plus original or scanned monthly utility bills (electricity, water, internet). Claims are processed on a reimbursement basis only. Accommodation must be a long-term residential property with proper rental documentation acceptable for accounting and audit.
✕
Non-Reimbursable ItemsSecurity deposits, advance rental payments, agency fees, furniture purchases, and personal expenses are not reimbursable unless specifically approved by management in advance.
!
Tax ResponsibilityAny personal income tax arising from accommodation benefits is the responsibility of the employee unless otherwise approved by management. Employees are advised to consult HR or a tax professional regarding personal tax obligations.
✓
Change of AccommodationEmployees wishing to change accommodation must notify HR in advance and follow the same submission process for the new rental agreement.
i
Reimbursable ExpensesActual transport costs, actual accommodation, and reasonable meal costs are reimbursable. All claims must reflect actual costs with proper documentation.
i
TransportDomestic: most economical mode. International: economy class. Local transport documented with receipts where possible.
!
Submit Within 7 Days of ReturnExpense claims must be submitted within 7 working days after the end of a trip or event. Late submissions may be rejected.
✕
No Receipt = No ReimbursementOriginal receipts required. Where unavailable, complete the "Expense Without Receipt" form with full justification — subject to Finance approval.
✓
Settle Outstanding AdvanceIf a cash advance was taken, deduct it from the claim. Any surplus must be returned to Finance at submission.
i
Processing TimeApproved payments processed within 3–5 business days after full sign-off. Urgent payments must be flagged to Finance in advance.
!
Original Invoice RequiredAll vendor payments require an original tax invoice. Photocopies or screenshots are not accepted without prior Finance approval.
i
Bank Transfer for Amounts ≥ 5,000 THBPayments of 5,000 THB or more must be via bank transfer. Cash is limited to petty cash items below this threshold.
✓
Withholding Tax (WHT)Finance will issue a WHT certificate for applicable vendor payments. Vendors should confirm their WHT rate when submitting invoices.
✕
No Splitting to Avoid ThresholdsSplitting a purchase into smaller transactions to avoid the CFO approval threshold is strictly prohibited.

HR Policies
Internal reference — Envisage Worldwide · Last updated 2026
🏖 Leave Policy
🏠 WFH Policy
📋 General Conduct
i
Annual Leave EntitlementStaff are entitled to annual leave as per their contract. Leave must be requested in advance and approved by the line manager before being taken.
!
Minimum Notice PeriodAnnual leave requires at least 3 working days' notice. Sick leave must be reported to the manager by 9:00 AM on the day, with a medical certificate for 3+ consecutive days.
✕
No Carry-Over Without ApprovalUnused annual leave does not automatically carry over unless approved in writing by management before year-end.
✓
Leave During ProbationStaff on probation may take leave only with manager approval. Emergency or medical leave is assessed case by case.
!
Public HolidaysThe organisation observes Thai public holidays as announced each year. Staff required to work on public holidays should confirm arrangements with HR in advance.
i
WFH EligibilityWork from home is subject to manager approval and role suitability. Approval is granted per request, not as a standing arrangement.
!
Submit in AdvanceWFH requests must be submitted and approved before the working day. Same-day requests are only considered in exceptional circumstances.
✓
Full Availability RequiredStaff working from home must be fully available during core hours, respond promptly, and meet all deliverables as if in the office.
✕
Not a Substitute for LeaveWFH may not be used in place of sick leave or annual leave. If unwell, apply for sick leave.
i
Professional StandardsAll staff must maintain professional conduct, punctuality, and respect for colleagues at all times — in the office or remotely.
!
ConfidentialityStaff must not share or disclose any confidential company, client, or project information without explicit written authorisation from management.
✕
Conflicts of InterestStaff may not engage in outside employment or business that conflicts with the organisation's interests without prior written approval from the Director.
✓
Grievances & FeedbackStaff with workplace concerns should raise them with their line manager or HR. The organisation is committed to addressing concerns fairly and confidentially.

💻 Asset Management
🏢 Office Rules
i
Company Asset ResponsibilityAll company equipment issued to staff remains the property of Envisage Worldwide. Staff are responsible for the safekeeping and proper use of all assigned assets.
!
Report Damage or Loss ImmediatelyAny damage, loss, or theft must be reported to Admin and the line manager within 24 hours. Delayed reporting may result in the cost being charged to the staff member.
✕
No Personal Use of Company AssetsCompany equipment must not be used for personal purposes, loaned to third parties, or removed from the office without prior approval.
✓
Return on SeparationAll company assets must be returned in good condition on or before the last working day. Outstanding assets will be recovered from the final salary.
i
Office HoursCore office hours are defined in the employment contract. Staff are expected to be present and available unless on approved leave or WFH.
!
Visitors & GuestsVisitors must sign in at reception. Staff are responsible for their guests while on company premises.
✓
Clean Desk PolicyStaff should maintain a tidy workspace. Confidential documents must be secured when leaving the desk. Shared spaces must be cleared after use.
✕
Food & DrinkFood and drink are restricted to the designated pantry and dining areas. Please clean up after yourself and do not leave food in shared spaces overnight.
